Partners

Partners for Real Change (PARC) is a voluntary non-governmental organization with a mandate to provide support and empower marginalized communities across the Rwenzori region of western Uganda. Started as an academic discussion group in 2003, it has evolved into a non-profit organization, whose formation was out of deep aspiration by a group of motivated youth to respond to the plight of the communities impacted by war.
