Welcome !

Mission Impassionible is a fiscally sponsored 501(c)3 student-run organization dedicated to providing

interdisciplinary opportunities to underrepresented groups through mentorship, education, resources, and workshops.

Our Activities

To educate, empower, and elevate

Mentorship

Qualified high school juniors, seniors, and undergraduate students will be paired with middle school students, high school freshmen, and sophomores to be mentored in the areas of writing, technology and science. By the end of the program the mentees will have a personalized project to call their own in a interdisciplinary subject.

Resource Center

Study guides, powerpoints, test banks, and videos will be produced as part of the Resource Center for students to access to enhance their academic studies.

Workshops

Professionals and qualified college students will share their experiences, expertise, and advice in virtual workshops/panels.
